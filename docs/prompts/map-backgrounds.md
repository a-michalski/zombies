## Level 1: First Contact

**Theme**: Coastal military outpost, first zombie encounter

**Scene Description**:
A small coastal defense installation, abandoned and showing early signs of the outbreak. Concrete fortifications overlook dark waters, with scattered military equipment and emergency barricades hastily erected. This is where the player first encounters the zombie threat.

**Visual Elements**:
- Concrete barriers and sandbag fortifications
- Rusted watchtower silhouettes in background
- Abandoned military crates and equipment
- Coastal rocks and dark water at edges
- Minimal vegetation (recent abandonment)
- Emergency floodlights (off/broken)

**AI Prompt (Copy-Paste Ready)**:
```
Create a top-down post-apocalyptic game map background, 640x384 pixels, for a mobile tower defense game.

SCENE: Abandoned coastal military bastion with concrete fortifications, watchtower ruins, and emergency barricades overlooking dark waters

STYLE: Dark gritty atmosphere, muted desaturated colors, base color #2a2a2a dark gray, post-apocalyptic military aesthetic, recent abandonment

PERSPECTIVE: Slight isometric angle (2.5D), top-down view, game map perspective

DETAILS: Concrete barriers and sandbags, scattered military equipment crates, rusted metal structures, coastal rocks at edges, dark water visible at margins, broken emergency lights, minimal overgrowth, rust and weathering, dramatic shadows from structures

LIGHTING: Dark ambient lighting with moonlight reflections on water, moody atmosphere, abandoned military installation feel

GROUND SURFACE: Concrete and asphalt, uniform military base texture, weathered but intact surface

CRITICAL REQUIREMENTS:
- Do NOT draw paths, roads, or routes on the background
- Paths will be added as separate overlay textures
- Keep the ground surface uniform and clear for gameplay
- Background decorations (barriers, crates, rocks) around edges and corners
- Center area should be relatively clear for tower and enemy placement
- Optimized for mobile performance
- PNG format, <500KB file size

PURPOSE: Background layer for tower defense game Level 1 (First Contact), enemies and towers will be rendered on top

TECHNICAL: 640x384px, PNG format, mobile-optimized, dark military theme (#2a2a2a base)
```

**Design Notes**:
This first level should feel ominous but not overwhelming. The military aesthetic establishes the defensive theme, while the coastal setting provides visual variety. Keep decorations minimal to avoid confusing new players - clear sight lines are important for the tutorial level.

## Implementation Notes

### Visual Progression Summary:
- **Levels 1-3**: Recent abandonment, minimal decay, mostly intact structures
- **Levels 4-6**: Moderate destruction, vegetation beginning to appear, significant damage
- **Levels 7-9**: Heavy destruction, nature aggressively reclaiming, structural collapse
- **Level 10**: Total apocalyptic devastation, no vegetation, maximum destruction

### Technical Reminders:
- All backgrounds: 640x384px, PNG format, <500KB target
- Base color: #2a2a2a (dark gray)
- Perspective: Slight isometric (2.5D), top-down view
- **CRITICAL**: No paths drawn on backgrounds - paths are separate overlays
- Clear center area for gameplay
- Decorative elements at edges and corners
- Optimized for mobile performance

### Color Palette Guidelines:
- **Base**: Dark gray (#2a2a2a)
- **Earth tones**: Browns, tans (muted)
- **Metal**: Grays, rust (orange-browns)
- **Vegetation**: Dark greens, yellows (desaturated)
- **Fire/Warning**: Orange, red (minimal accent use)
- **Water**: Dark blue-gray
- **Overall**: Desaturated, moody, dark

### AI Tool Tips:
- **Midjourney**: Add `--ar 5:3 --style raw --v 6` for best results
- **DALL-E 3**: Copy prompt directly, may need to emphasize "top-down view"
- **Stable Diffusion**: May need additional negative prompts: "cartoon, bright colors, cel-shaded"
