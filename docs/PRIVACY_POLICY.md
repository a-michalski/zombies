# Privacy Policy for Zombie Fleet Bastion

**Effective Date:** November 17, 2025
**Last Updated:** November 17, 2025

## Introduction

Finelab Adam Michalski ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Zombie Fleet Bastion mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

## Information We Collect

### Data Stored Locally on Your Device

Zombie Fleet Bastion stores the following information locally on your device using AsyncStorage:

#### Game Statistics
- Best wave reached
- Total zombies killed
- Total waves survived
- Number of games played

#### Settings & Preferences
- Music enabled/disabled
- Sound effects enabled/disabled
- Default game speed preference

#### Campaign Progress
- Unlocked levels
- Level completion status
- Star ratings for completed levels
- Total stars earned
- Total scrap earned in campaign mode

**Important:** All data is stored locally on your device only. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of this information on external servers.

## How We Use Your Information

The data stored on your device is used solely for:

1. **Game Functionality** - To save your progress, settings, and statistics
2. **Personalization** - To remember your preferences (audio settings, game speed)
3. **Progress Tracking** - To track your achievements and campaign progress

## Data Sharing and Transmission

**We do not share, sell, or transmit your data to third parties.**

Zombie Fleet Bastion operates entirely offline and does not:
- Send data to external servers
- Share information with advertisers
- Transmit analytics to third-party services
- Require internet connectivity

## Data Security

Your data is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage, which is:
- Protected by your device's security measures
- Not accessible to other applications
- Only accessible while the app is installed

## Data Retention

Your data is retained on your device until:
- You uninstall the app
- You manually reset your progress using the "Reset Progress" option in Settings
- You clear the app's data through your device settings

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

### Access Your Data
All data stored by the app is visible within the app interface (statistics, settings, campaign progress).

### Delete Your Data
You can delete your data by:
1. Using the "Reset Progress" option in the Settings menu
2. Uninstalling the app
3. Clearing the app's data through your device settings

### Export Your Data
You can view all your data within the app. If you need to export your data, you can use the "Export Data" feature in Settings (if available) or manually record the information displayed in the app.

## Children's Privacy

Zombie Fleet Bastion is rated for ages 9+ and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app:
- Does not require account creation
- Does not collect personal information
- Stores only game-related data locally on the device

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will:
- Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
- Notify users through the app (if changes are significant)

Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

## Third-Party Services

Zombie Fleet Bastion does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.
